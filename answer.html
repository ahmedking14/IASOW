Project Overview

Impact Applications is a full‑service grant consulting firm helping Canadian businesses secure funding. Their team expertly researches, writes and manages grant applications but keeping up with constantly changing programs and opportunities manually can be time‑consuming and lead to missed funding.

This proposal outlines the design and deployment of an intelligent Grant Alert & Eligibility Agent. The automation will continuously monitor federal, provincial and municipal funding databases, newsletters and public announcements to surface new grants. It will cross‑reference each opportunity against your clients’ profiles to determine eligibility and deliver personalized alerts.

Strategic Value

Ensure no opportunity slips through the cracks. Capture emerging programs as soon as they’re announced and deliver timely, relevant grant alerts directly to your clients.

Time Savings

Automate 2–3 hours of weekly research per client and free your team to focus on high‑value relationship management and grant writing.

Revenue Growth

Increase your clients’ success rates by matching them with more funding opportunities, driving greater project budgets and higher fees.

Detailed Deliverables

We deliver a comprehensive solution including back‑end automation, data management, client communications and future scalability.

Grant Discovery Bot

Custom scrapers search multiple public and private databases, news feeds and newsletters for new grant programs on a daily basis. Flexible parsing extracts program name, funder, budget, deadline and key criteria.

Eligibility Engine

Intelligent rules evaluate each grant against your client database, considering industry, location, company size, revenue, export capabilities and project scope. Assign eligibility scores and tag priority leads.

Client Data Hub

Design and implement a centralized data repository (Google Sheets or integrated CRM) to store detailed client profiles. Build secure connectors to Monday.com or your existing onboarding platform.

Automated Alerts

Generate polished, personalized email summaries of new grants and send them weekly or on demand. Include grant summaries, eligibility rating and next‑steps call‑to‑action.

Admin Dashboard

Provide an internal dashboard to monitor scraping health, review upcoming grant deadlines, adjust eligibility parameters and track grant conversion metrics.

Training & Support

Deliver comprehensive documentation and training for your team. Two live onboarding sessions ensure everyone is comfortable maintaining and extending the automation.

Implementation Timeline

The project is structured over approximately six weeks. We believe in rapid iteration and will deliver tangible results each week.

Week 1

Discovery & Planning

Kickoff meeting to map existing workflows, confirm data fields and identify target grant sources. Define success metrics and finalize technical architecture.

Week 2

Database & Integration

Build client data hub in Google Sheets or connect to Monday CRM. Import existing client profiles and set up secure API connections.

Week 3

Scraper Development

Develop scraping scripts and integrate with grant databases, newsletters and RSS feeds. Normalize extracted data into unified format.

Week 4

Eligibility Algorithm

Create eligibility logic based on company size, industry, revenue, incorporation date and project scope. Test with sample client profiles and refine scoring thresholds.

Week 5

Notification System

Design templated email digest and schedule sending triggers. Implement UI for managing frequency (daily/weekly) and customizing recipients.

Week 6

Testing & Deployment

End‑to‑end testing with real data; iterate based on feedback. Deploy automation to production environment and host final training sessions.

Client Requirements

To ensure a smooth deployment, the following items are needed from your team:

Client Data Export

Provide a spreadsheet or CRM export of all current clients with key fields: industry, location, employee count, revenues, project plans and any past grant history.

System Access

Grant API access or credentials for Monday.com or other CRMs, motion.io onboarding platform and email marketing tools for integration.

Eligibility Rules

Share any predefined eligibility guidelines or checklists your team uses to vet grants so we can codify them into the algorithm.

Communication Preferences

Decide on alert frequency (daily, weekly), preferred notification channels (email, Slack) and designate points of contact for feedback.

Scheduling

Select dates for discovery workshops and training sessions. Participation from technical and operational stakeholders is essential.
